German Shepherd

The German Shepherd is a breed of working dog originating in Germany. Used for a variety of tasks, including herding, police work, and search and rescue, they are known for their intelligence, loyalty, and athleticism.

Belgian Malinois

The Belgian Malinois is a breed of herding and working dog, often used in police and military work. They are known for their intense drive, focus, and agility.

Doberman Pinscher

The Doberman Pinscher is a medium-large breed of domestic dog developed in Germany in the 1890s. Intelligent, loyal, and often used as guard dogs, they are known for their cropped ears and docked tails .

Lhasa Apso

The Lhasa Apso is an ancient breed of dog from Tibet, often used as a sentinel in palaces and monasteries. They are small, loyal, and known for their long, luxurious coats.

Czech Shepherd

As mentioned earlier, there is no dog breed officially recognized as the "Czech Shepherd." The closest breed is the Czechoslovakian Wolfdog, a German Shepherd and Carpathian wolf hybrid bred for military and police use, with high energy and intelligence levels.

Belgian Shepherd

The Belgian Shepherd is a versatile breed with four varieties, including the long-haired black Groenendael and the short-haired fawn Malinois, used for herding, assistance, police, and military work.

Australian Shepherd

The Australian Shepherd, commonly referred to as the "Aussie," is a working breed known for their intelligence, agility, and high energy levels, often used as herding or assistance dogs.

Parson Russell Terrier

The Parson Russell Terrier, also known as the Jack Russell Terrier, is a small, lively breed with a high energy level and a keen hunting instinct. They make great companion pets.

Pit Bull

It is important to note that "Pit Bull" is not a breed of dog, but rather an umbrella term used to refer to several breeds and their mixes, including American Pit Bull Terriers, American Staffordshire Terriers, and Staffordshire Bull Terriers.

Russian Caucasian Shepherd Dog

The Russian Caucasian Shepherd Dog, also known as the Caucasian Ovcharka, is a large, powerful breed originally used for guarding livestock. They are intelligent, independent, and loyal to their family.

Schnauzers

The Schnauzer is a German breed of dog with a distinctive bearded snout. They come in three sizes: Standard, Giant, and Miniature. They are known for their intelligence and independence.

Black German Shepherd

The Black German Shepherd is a color variation of the German Shepherd breed, with a black coat and typical German Shepherd traits of loyalty, intelligence, and protective instincts.
