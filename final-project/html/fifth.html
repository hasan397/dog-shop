Great Dane

Great Danes are a German breed of dog that are known for their large size and friendly nature. They have a lifespan of around 7-10 years and come in various coat colors.

English Mastiff

The English Mastiff is a giant dog breed that originated in Britain. They have a short coat, massive head, and can weigh up to 230 pounds. They are known for their loyal and gentle nature, but may be prone to certain health issues.

Saint Bernard

The Saint Bernard is a giant breed of dog that originated in the Western Alps of Italy and Switzerland. They were bred for rescue work by the hospice of the Great St Bernard Pass and are known for their gentle and patient temperament.

Irish Wolfhound

The Irish Wolfhound is a large breed of sighthound from Ireland, known for its size and personal quirks. They have a relatively short lifespan and may be prone to certain health issues such as dilated cardiomyopathy.

Neapolitan Mastiff

The Neapolitan Mastiff is a giant breed of dog from Italy known for its distinctive wrinkled appearance and loyal nature. They have a short, dense coat and are prone to certain health issues such as hip dysplasia and bloat.

Leonberger

The Leonberger is a giant breed of dog from Germany, known for its size and strength. They have a thick, waterproof coat and are loyal and intelligent. They require proper training and care due to their size and potential health issues.

Tibetan Mastiff

The Tibetan Mastiff is a large, double-coated breed from Tibet, known for its protective and independent nature. They were originally used to guard monasteries and are a primitive breed.

Newfoundland

The Newfoundland is a giant breed of dog from Canada, known for its thick, waterproof coat and webbed feet. They were bred for water rescue and are excellent swimmers. They have a friendly and loyal temperament.

Anatolian Shepherd

The Anatolian Shepherd is a large breed of dog from Turkey, known for its protective nature and history as a livestock guardian. They have a short, dense coat and can weigh up to 150 pounds.

Scottish Deerhound

this dog is a regal and athletic breed of dog from Scotland. They are tall and slender, standing at 28-32 inches at the shoulder, and have a crisp. They are affectionate and gentle but require ample exercise and space to move around.

Dogue de Bordeaux

The Dogue de Bordeaux is a large French mastiff breed known for its powerful build , massive head, and short, fine coat. They typically have a well-tempered personality but can be prone to health issues and have a shorter life expectancy.

Great Pyrenees

This dog is a large breed of dog that originated in France as a livestock guardian. They have a thick, white coat and are known for their independent and protective nature. They can weigh up to 100-120 pounds and stand up to 32 inches at the shoulder.
